About Ginuo Musica

We are a team of music scholars and professionals into teaching of music theory, voice coaching and training on musical instruments. We prepare students for musical performances, competitions and graded local/international exams. We are organizers of musical concerts and performances. We provide chamber-music and orchestra / band; performance of solos, duets, quartets for voice and/or strings, woodwind and brass instruments. We also edit and publish musical works. We are also into sale of musical instruments, music books, music manuscripts and training materials.

We are the conveners of the National Youth Orchestras of Nigeria, a team of music professionals into training on musical instruments & voice coaching, sale of musical instruments and performance.
